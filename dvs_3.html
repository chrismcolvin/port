DVS - Rehau

Rehau makes beautiful windows, doors and cabinetry using advanced polymer solutions.

Challenge

Rehau, a client of DVS, was looking to get photography and video work done for an upcoming ad campaign. DVS turned to me to capture the machinery and the people of Rehau.

Approach

While the video team did their thing, I focused on individuals doing their work in the shop. I really wanted to show that these people cared about their product.

Create

The light in the shop was actually great for this shoot. Using minimal external lighting and a simple kit, I was able to shoot fast and get into some really interesting angles.
