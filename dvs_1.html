DVS - Workspace

Located just south-west of downtown Grand Rapids, DVS's workspace is a beautiful renovated factory building with tons of character.

Challenge

DVS was looking for a photographer to do get some shots showing off the beauty and atmosphere of their awesome office to use on their website.

Approach

With tons of natural light, I wanted to capture the space in a way that would show off the huge windows and the old city feel.

Create

With such a cool space, I was able to simply wonder the office, and capture things as I saw them. It was really cool and super fun!
