DVS - WMLHS

A small high school located in West Michigan dedicated to a personal and Christian learning environment.

Challenge

Capture the students and their enviroment at WMLHS to use for a website redesign.

Approach

I wanted to capture the students doing what they would do normally - how they interacted with each other and with their teachers. I also wanted to show some of the creative and stimulating learning environment that WMLHS provides.

Create

Spending a day with the students and teachers, I was able to follow and capture what happened throughout the day. It was a really cool experience, and the students were really able to get comfortable with me being there, which allowed me to capture them doing what they would normally do.
