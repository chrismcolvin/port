About Me

My name is Chris, and I love making cool stuff out of pixels.

I discovered Graphic Design late in high school, and was immediately hooked. That’s when I decided to go to college for design. In college I discovered web development, and quickly found a love for all things code. Since then I have been honing my coding and web design skills to build the best stuff I can.
In my free time I enjoy PC gaming, dreaming about tech, cruisin’ on my fixie, or spending time with my wife and dog. I relish these times, and try to make the most of them.
